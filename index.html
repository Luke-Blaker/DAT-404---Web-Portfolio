About me

Hi. My name is Chris.

Phat Digital is my way of offering Simple and Straightforward Web Development Services to those looking for a jargon free and clean cut service.

After spending the last 3.5 years as part of a larger agency (Tellonline) I am now continuing my career in the world of Web Development on my own, focusing mainly on delivering solutions to start ups, and those who aren't quite sure what a presence on the web can do for them and their business.

Wordpress is my platform of choice when it comes to CMS solutions, but I have experience working in Kentico, Joomla and delivering Static HTML pages, where necessary.
